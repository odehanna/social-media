Area Handbooks are go-to reference guides for open source information on critical locations. From population distribution to critical people and recent events, Area Handbooks provide users with baseline information to validate and caveat their findings.

SecDev’s Area Handbooks place extra emphasis on telecommunications capabilities, enabling a more accurate characterization of the information space. In general, people can’t use social media if they don’t have access to the internet. SecDev uses ITU data and international reporting to provide context and perspective on user-generated content.
